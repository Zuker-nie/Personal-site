About Me

Who are you?

Hello, my name is Zuker Nie Chen and I am an aspiring Software Developer. I am currently a Computer Science Major at York University.

How did you get into Computer Programming?

It is a very funny story because it started with a course from High School taught by one of my favorite professors, we were learning to make simple C# games and from then on I realized I really enjoyed coding.

What drives you?

The idea that inspires me to keep working in this field stems from my parents, they came from nothing and were able to provide me a wonderful life and I want to be able to repay them by showng them that I will become succesful. Luckily for me, my parents were open enough to let me make my choices in terms of careers and with this choice, I want to let them know I made the right choice.

Where are you from?

I was born in Caracas, Venezuela. I then was moved back to China to study there from age 1 to 5 and then moved back to Venezuela again. From there I lived in Venezuela for 6 years and moved to Toronto, Canada at the age of 11 and I have been living here ever since.

What are your strengths?

One of my greatest strengths is good communication skills while working in a group. I strive to deliver independent work and be able to contribute to the group to find optimal solutions to problems.

What are your interests?

Aside from starting new projects with friends, I enjoy being indoors either playing video games at a competitive level or exploring applications and analyzing features that are interesting to me. Outdoor activities that I enjoy include basketball, badminton and occasinal tennis. At least once a week, I get in a good workout either cardio or weightlifting at the gym near my home.
